What happens when you “Open Command Deck”

The Command Deck is your entry point into SSAG. It starts with a short application so we can match you to the right path.

Step 1 – Tell us about you

  • Share your location, experience level, and how soon you want to get moving.
  • Tell us if you’re interested in one business, a territory, or a larger play.
  • Let us know your approximate launch budget and type of support you want.

Step 2 – We match you to a track

  • We review your answers and align you with Digital Pack, Operator Kit, City License, or Investor.
  • If there’s a fit, you’ll be invited to a call or message-based walkthrough.
  • You’ll see exactly what you’re getting before you commit.
