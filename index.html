How SSAG works

Simple path from “interested” to operating a real business in your city.

1. Choose your path

  • Pick one business you want to start with, or a small bundle.
  • Decide if you want to operate yourself, partner with others, or scale to multiple areas.
  • Use the application form to share your goals and location.

2. Apply & connect

  • Fill out the short SSAG form with your city, skills, and budget.
  • We review fit for the type of business and territory you want.
  • If it’s a match, we walk you through the next steps and options.

3. Get systems & support

  • Receive guides, checklists, and customer scripts for your chosen business.
  • Use simple tools to book jobs, respond to leads, and keep track of work.
  • Grow at your own pace, with options to add more services over time.

4. Expand when you’re ready

  • Stay focused on one area or add more SSAG services in your region.
  • Turn successful services into territory-based or city-level opportunities.
  • Build a long-term local brand backed by multiple income streams.
