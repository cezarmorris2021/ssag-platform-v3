Command Deck

The SSAG Command Deck is the control surface where you route deals, operators, and investors into the right tracks.

Use this page as the shell

  • Drop in your own payment buttons, checkout links, or proposal forms.
  • Connect Calendly or any scheduling link for operator and investor calls.
  • Link to private Notion docs, Google Drive folders, or data rooms.
  • Route deals based on whether someone is a license buyer, operator, or investor.

What plugs in behind the scenes

  • CRMs and pipelines (HubSpot, GoHighLevel, Close, etc.).
  • Email sequences for launch, onboarding, and ongoing updates.
  • Automation bots that send playbooks, templates, or contracts.
  • Shared folders that contain the actual business-in-a-box systems.

Operator track

Frame SSAG as a path for operators who want a done-with-you business system.

  • Simple intake form asking about market, budget, and timeline.
  • Call or message flow that qualifies if they’re a fit for a territory.
  • Offer ladder: digital packs → local operator kits → full city license.
  • Optional: performance-based deals or revenue share per territory.

Investor & partner track

Show how investors plug capital into territories, systems, or portfolio deals.

  • Overview of how SSAG creates multiple streams from a single platform.
  • Explain where funds go: systems, operators, marketing, and expansion.
  • Outline example deal structures: equity, revenue share, or notes.
  • Point to a private data room or deck when you have one ready.
